Spoiler Shield Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026. This policy explains what data Spoiler Shield handles and why.

What Spoiler Shield Does

Spoiler Shield blurs spoiler text on webpages based on the keywords you add in the extension popup. You can reveal any blurred text with a click.

Single purpose
User-controlled keywords
On-device processing

Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. We do not track browsing history, URLs, or user activity.

Your keyword list is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local so it persists between sessions.

Permissions Used

Storage is used to save your spoiler keywords locally. No other permissions are requested.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics, ads, or remote servers involved.

Policy Updates

If this policy changes, we will update this page with a new effective date.
